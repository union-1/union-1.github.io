Union-1

The project is based on philosophical foundations and practical implementation of the concept: "everyone should do what he wants, love and respect his neighbor, and not interfere with the rest." The practical implementation is primarily carried out through the creation of more profitable and comfortable working conditions for the masters of their favorite craft. The project claims to be a turquoise organization.

Union-1 is creating ideal conditions for the happy existence of its members. Union-1 is a concept of ideal conditions for a happy life, based on the belief of the participants in their manifesto (see below).

Members of the union unite in organizations and work on a common cause to realize their mission for the benefit of themselves, other members of the organization and the world outside of it.

The organizations of the Union-1 help and accept help from other organizations of the Union-1, that is, they support "their own", "allied" ones, and by their activities for a decent payment they benefit people and organizations outside the Union-1, due to which they get rich organization, each of its members individually and the entire Union-1.

A member of the union goes to a happy, bright and eventful life, through successes and achievements in his favorite business, through love for friends and relatives, through the realization of his and their dreams.

The goal of Union-1 is to help everyone understand what they want to do, what to create, what to love, find an opportunity for this and use it.

Means of the Union-1 - the creation of communities, unions, organizations. Providing them with information, technological, financial and organizational support.

Products of members and organizations of Union-1 are goods, services and creations created by happy people who love their job and do their job well.
